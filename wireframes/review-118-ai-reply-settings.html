Design Notes
1
Entry point: "AI Reply Settings" is a 3rd item in the Settings sidebar — same style as "Profile Information" and "Password & Security" above it.
2
Business dropdown: Lists all businesses of the user. Businesses with saved instructions show "✓ Saved". Switching the dropdown reloads the textarea with that business's saved data (or blank if none).
3
Textarea: Free-text, multi-line, max 1000 characters. Live character counter below (turns red when over limit). Save button is disabled if over 1000 chars.
4
Save Changes: Shows loading spinner on click → success toast → data saved. Reuse the existing settings page button style.
5
RTL / Arabic: Full mirror — labels align right, dropdown chevron flips, layout reverses. Must be validated in Arabic locale.
6
Mobile: Settings sub-nav collapses to a tab bar or accordion. All fields stack full-width. Save button goes full-width at the bottom.
